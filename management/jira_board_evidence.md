## Project Management Setup

### Jira Configuration
- Project key: AIRIS (or similar)
- Board type: Scrum
- Team members: Assigned and visible
- Backlog: Organized by sprints

### Board Structure
- Backlog: Product backlog items
- To Do: Sprint tasks to start
- In Progress: Active work items
- In Review: Completed, awaiting review
- Done: Completed and accepted

### Tracking Metrics
- Velocity tracking across sprints
- Burndown charts generated
- Story points estimated and tracked
- Sprint dates recorded
